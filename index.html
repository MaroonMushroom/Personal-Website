Sabine de Leeuw

LL.M. | Legal Professional - Legal Tech with EIFFEL | Full stack web developer in training

My name is Sabine de Leeuw. 'Stay ahead, don't just keep up' is mine and EIFFEL's mantra. My specialism is to seek connection between legal professionals and software developers. The goal is not to replace legal professionals by computers; but to get software to take over the repetitive and administrative tasks of the legal field. This way legal professionals are enabled to put more of their specialism into work that cannot be performed by tech.
